What Am I Looking At?

You're looking at an experimental project designed by the NPR Apps team and the Social Media team at NPR.

As a newsroom, we don't report facts until they are verified. We wanted to let you see what we do know, how we know what we know, and what we're actively working on verifying during breaking news events.

Inspiration: The Makah people of the Pacific Northwest have evidence built into their verb tenses. You can't grammatically build a sentence without explaining how you know what you know.
