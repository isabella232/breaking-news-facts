About This Project

The epistemology of breaking news: We're looking to put together a single digital source of truth for "what we know to be true" during breaking news events. What do we know? And why do we know what we know? We'll focus on attribution, transparency, aggregation and fact-checking. We aim to deliver a usable prototype.

Aside: Did you know that the Makah people of the Pacific Northwest have evidence built in to their verb tenses? http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Makah_language#Morphology You literally can't grammatically build a sentence without explaining how you know what you know.
